Gmail Random Quotes

Thank you for installing Random Quotes!

To start, let's add some quotes to the list below. Feel free to add your own quotes, or Google for some cool ones. Copy and paste those quotes below, one by line.

Once you have some quotes, when you compose an email on Gmail, a random quote from the list should be automatically added to the email.

If you are experience issues, please contact support.

IMPORTANT: There is a small delay of about 3 seconds for the quote to be added to the message. The reason for that is to make sure the entire element has been correctly rendered in the browser. This might change in future versions.
